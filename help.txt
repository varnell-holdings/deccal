Help for importing .csv files into Googlr Calendar.



On the left mid way down you should see DEC Roster with a red check next to it.

To the right of that there are three vertical dots.

Click and choose Setting and Sharing.

In the Settings page you will see three down from the top left 'Import & export'.

Click and now you get 'Import' -> 'Select file from your computer'

Choose D:\JOHN TILLET\source\calendar\files_for_google\'the one you want'

IMPORTANT     below that in 'Add to calendar' choose 'DEC Roster'

Click Import

It will run and you are done for another three months!
